How to get there

Studio Zena
Borneokade 68, 1019 AW Amsterdam

https://maps.app.goo.gl/hr7wbRZNuzdYVMhw7

Bus 43 from Amsterdam Centraal to R.J.H. Fortuynplein every 20 minutes

Before: you will see a boat with a mermaid statue in front.

Take steps up and right onto the boat, take right (towards the street) after boarding.

Take first turn to the left, and you will see a hatch to your right.
