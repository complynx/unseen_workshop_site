How to get there

Studio Zena
Borneokade 68, 1019 AW Amsterdam

Open in Google Maps

By bus from Amsterdam Centraal: take bus 43 and get off at R.J.H. Fortuynplein (buses run about every 20 minutes). After getting off, face the same direction the bus was driving, then turn right onto R.J.H. Fortuynplein and continue through. The mermaid statue should be in front of you.

You will see a boat with a mermaid statue in front.

Enter the passage on the pier to the left of the pillar.

Walk up the steps and board the boat. After boarding, turn right (towards the street).

Take the first left turn. You will see a hatch on your right.

Step down through the hatch into the boat.
